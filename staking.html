FAQ's

How to stake NYM?

  1. Download NYM wallet from nymtech.net/download.
  2. If you already have an account, you simply need to sign in. Otherwise, you will need to follow the needed steps to create one.
  3. Create an account or sign in.
  4. On the left side of the wallet, in the menu, you will find an option called "Delegate". Please click on it.
  5. The next steps would be to enter our mix node identity address (you can find above) and fill in the amount you want to stake with us.

Congratulations! You are now officially staking your NYM and started earning rewards. We are excited to have you join us.
