Why us

We use quality hardware located in a high-performance data centers. Our infrastructure is fully automated so even in the event of large-scale failure at the data center our servers can be restored in minutes. This limits potential downtime to bare minimum.

Our servers are located in a small & secure datacenters, not dependent on the infrastructure of large cloud providers.

For responsible staking we recommend that you delegate across several mixnodes. By doing so you not only increase your chances of a higher return but also help diversify - and therefore strengthen - the Nym network. We hope you choose Nym Watch for a portion of your delegation.
